Auto Injury Treatment

Expert chiropractic care for whiplash, neck pain, back injuries, and more. If you've been in a car accident — don't wait. Early treatment makes all the difference.

Injured in a Car Accident?

Car accidents are one of the most common causes of musculoskeletal injury — and one of the most misunderstood. Even a low-speed collision can generate enough force to strain muscles, misalign the spine, and damage the soft tissues in your neck and back.

What makes auto injuries especially tricky is that symptoms often don't appear right away. Adrenaline, inflammation delays, and gradual tissue swelling can mask pain for hours — or even days — after the crash. By the time you feel it, the injury may already be progressing.

Dr. Wright has extensive experience evaluating and treating auto-related injuries. The sooner you're assessed, the better your recovery outcome.

Don't wait to get checked. Many injuries worsen without treatment — early care is the key to a full recovery.

Common Auto Injuries We Treat

Car accident injuries can affect the entire body, but the most common involve the spine, neck, and surrounding soft tissues. Dr. Wright evaluates and treats a wide range of auto-related conditions, including:

  • Whiplash
  • Neck pain and stiffness
  • Upper and lower back pain
  • Headaches following injury
  • Shoulder and arm pain
  • Muscle tension and spasms
  • Reduced range of motion
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Jaw pain (TMJ)
  • Dizziness or vertigo
  • Fatigue and difficulty concentrating
  • Spinal misalignment

If you're experiencing any of these symptoms after an accident — even if they seem minor — it's worth getting evaluated. What feels like mild soreness can sometimes indicate a more significant underlying injury.

Why Early Treatment Matters

The window right after an accident is critical. Inflammation and micro-tears in muscles and ligaments can harden into scar tissue if left untreated — leading to chronic pain, restricted movement, and long-term complications that are much harder to address down the road.

Early chiropractic intervention helps in several important ways:

  • Reduces inflammation before it becomes chronic
  • Restores proper spinal alignment
  • Prevents scar tissue formation
  • Relieves pain without medication
  • Speeds up the body's natural healing process
  • Documents your injuries for insurance purposes

Many auto injury cases are covered by personal injury protection (PIP) insurance, meaning you may be able to receive care with little or no out-of-pocket cost. Dr. Wright's office can help guide you through the process.

Dr. Wright's Approach to Auto Injury Care

Dr. Wright begins with a thorough evaluation to understand the full scope of your injuries. He takes the time to listen to your experience, assess your spine and soft tissues, and identify any areas of dysfunction that may not yet be symptomatic.

Rather than a one-size-fits-all approach, Dr. Wright tailors each treatment plan to the individual — taking into account the type of accident, the forces involved, your current symptoms, and your health history. His goal is not just to manage pain, but to address the root cause of your injury and help your body heal correctly from the inside out.

With over 30 years of experience serving the Payson, Utah community, Dr. Wright brings a calm, careful, and patient-centered approach to every visit.

Treatment Methods

Dr. Wright uses a combination of gentle, proven chiropractic techniques to treat auto injuries effectively:

  • KST (Koren Specific Technique)
  • Spinal adjustments and realignment
  • Soft tissue therapy
  • Muscle relaxation techniques
  • Posture and movement guidance
  • Corrective care planning

KST, Dr. Wright's primary technique, uses a specialized instrument to deliver precise, gentle adjustments without any twisting, cracking, or popping. This makes it especially well-suited for patients who are sensitive or in acute pain following an accident.
