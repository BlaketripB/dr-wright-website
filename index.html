Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Wright

For over 30 years, Dr. Wright has been a trusted name in chiropractic care throughout the Payson, Utah community. His passion for helping people live pain-free lives drives everything he does.

What sets Dr. Wright apart is his approach. He doesn't believe in the traditional "rack and crack" method. Instead, he takes the time to carefully assess your body, identify exactly what's out of alignment, and deliver gentle, precise adjustments right where they're needed.

KST (Koren Specific Technique) — A gentle, instrument-based approach that allows Dr. Wright to analyze your body's structure and deliver highly specific corrections without the twisting, popping, or cracking associated with traditional chiropractic methods.

Patients describe Dr. Wright as genuinely friendly and deeply caring — the kind of doctor who listens, takes his time, and treats every person like family.
